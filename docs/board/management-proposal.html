Executive Summary

Short Call Web is a lightweight platform to coordinate urgent shift-based staffing requests across BC Place departments. It improves staffing speed, queue transparency, and role-based governance with Schedule approval control.

Business Problem

  • Slow staffing response during high-variability shifts.
  • Low transparency in candidate ordering and outcomes.
  • Inconsistent governance across departments.
  • Limited traceability for management review.

Proposed Solution

  1. Manager/Admin submits short-call request.
  2. Schedule reviews and approves/rejects publication.
  3. Eligible employees apply.
  4. Queue is ranked by timestamp.
  5. Status is visible: pending / confirmed / waitlisted.

Target Operating Model

RoleResponsibilities
EmployeeApply to eligible calls and track queue/status.
ManagerCreate requests and monitor demand fulfilment.
ScheduleApprove/reject requests, control frequency balancing.
AdminCross-role governance and oversight.
