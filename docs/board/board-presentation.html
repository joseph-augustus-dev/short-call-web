Board of Management Briefing

BC Place staffing agility and governance modernization

Why this matters now

  • Shift demand volatility is increasing.
  • Manual short-call coordination is slow and opaque.
  • Governance consistency is required across departments.

Current pain points

  • Delayed publishing and mobilization.
  • Limited transparency in queue fairness.
  • Low traceability for leadership review.

Proposed workflow

  1. Manager/Admin creates request.
  2. Schedule approves/rejects.
  3. Employees apply.
  4. Queue ranked by timestamp.
  5. Status visible to all roles.

Strategic outcomes

  • Faster staffing response.
  • Improved fill-rate for critical shifts.
  • Transparent, governed decisioning.

Decision requested

  1. Approve pilot authorization.
  2. Approve production foundation investment.
  3. Approve monthly KPI governance cadence.
